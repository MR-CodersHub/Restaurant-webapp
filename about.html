Our Journey & Craft

Our Story

Founded on the principles of authenticity and warmth, Woods & Spices was born from a passion for the slow-cooked, smoky flavors of traditional hearth cooking.

What started as a small family kitchen has evolved into a sanctuary for food lovers. Every dish we serve is a tribute to the craftsmanship of the past.

Our Philosophy

Sustainability

We work exclusively with local farmers who practice regenerative agriculture, ensuring every ingredient respects the earth.

Authenticity

No shortcuts. We use traditional wood-fire techniques and hand-ground spices to preserve the integrity of flavor.

Community

Food is connection. We strive to create a space where memories are made and culture is celebrated.

A Decade of Flavor

From a humble food truck to a culinary landmark.

2015

The Spark

Woods & Spices begins as a pop-up kitchen in Chennai, experimenting with smoked meats and traditional masalas.

2018

First Location

We opened our doors in Mylapore, quickly gaining a reputation for our signature "Smoked Ribs & Saffron Biryani".

2022

Culinary Award

Awarded "Best Fusion Restaurant" by the City Food Guide, recognizing our innovative approach to flavor.

2026

New Horizons

Expanding our menu and renovating our space to offer an even more immersive dining experience.
